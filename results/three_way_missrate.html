Three-way degradation: FlexTrack-V2 vs XTrack vs SeqTrackv2

Tracking accuracy as the auxiliary modality (thermal / event / depth) is progressively dropped at inference (0→100%), on four RGB-X benchmarks, full test sets, each benchmark's official metric, identical missing masks across trackers. R0 reproduces each tracker's reported SOTA.

Conclusion

FlexTrack-V2 is the most robust to modality absence on every benchmark — both the highest full-modality (R0) accuracy and the smallest drop to RGB-only.

Full-modality → RGB-only drop (success metric): FlexTrack-V2 loses the least on all four; XTrack degrades the steepest (RGBT234 −9.3 MSR, LasHeR −11.6 SR); SeqTrackv2 sits between.

Modality matters by dataset: thermal (LasHeR) and depth (DepthTrack) are load-bearing — all three trackers drop most there; the event stream (VisEvent) is near-redundant given RGB — all three degrade gently.
